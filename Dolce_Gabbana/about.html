Dolce & Gabbana is an Italian luxury fashion house, founded in 1985 in Legnano. Founders are two Italian designers named Domenico Dolce and Stefano Gabbana. The two met in Milan in 1980 and designed for the same fashion house. In 1982, they established a designer consulting studio; in time it grew to become "Dolce & Gabbana". They presented their first women's collection in 1985 in Milan, where a year later their store would open its doors.
